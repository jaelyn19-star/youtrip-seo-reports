Fix breadcrumb schema "Unnamed item" bug — sitewide
Sitewide Schema / Technical ~1–2 hours → See Indexing report

Rewrite Singapore Public Holiday 2026 title and meta description
351K impressions · 0.32% CTR CTR Optimisation → See Traffic report

This page sits at position 5 for a ~90,000 volume keyword but converts just 0.32% of impressions to clicks — the lowest CTR of any top-position page in the audit. SERP features (featured snippets, calendar cards) are dominating the visible space. A stronger, more compelling title and meta can help carve out a higher share of the remaining click pool.

  • Current title is likely generic (e.g. "Singapore Public Holidays 2026"). Test a more specific variant: "Singapore Public Holidays 2026 — Full Calendar with Long Weekends"

  • Track CTR weekly in GSC after the change — allow 2–3 weeks for Googlebot to pick up the new meta
